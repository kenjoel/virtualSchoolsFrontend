ABOUT VIRTUAL SCHOOLS

Our Mission

Virtual School's mission is to help people learn the skills they need to get great jobs. For us, “great jobs”
means jobs in growing industries that pay well and provide rewarding work. Beyond the particular skills needed
to get these jobs, we aim to help our students become confident self-teachers who can adapt to changing job markets,
and great communicators who will work well in teams. We focus on serving people who, by birth or circumstance, don't
have easy access to learning the skills they need to get great jobs.



Our Story

Our scrum master, Ken Joel, founded Virtual Schools in 2020. After becoming frustrated with how difficult it was
