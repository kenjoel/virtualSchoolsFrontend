Classes Online

All of our classes are offered online as well as in-person! Much like our in-person classes, we emphasize collaboration and community in our online classes. We start each class with a video standup, and then students pair up and work together on projects. Classes communicate on Discord (a text and audio chat app), and pairs use Discord to talk with each other while they work. Teachers join each pair throughout the class to check in, give feedback, and troubleshoot. All of our policies, including tuition, are the same for our online and in-person classes, except when noted.
